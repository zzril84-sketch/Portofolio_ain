About Me

Professional Background

A brief overview of my academic background, professional competencies, and what drives me as an Engineering Designer.

I am Muhamad Nadzril Ain, a graduate of S1 Sistem Komputer (Bachelor of Computer Systems) from Universitas Pamulang. My academic background has equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of computer architecture, programming, hardware-software integration, and system analysis.

As an Engineering Designer, I bridge the gap between technical engineering requirements and visual design execution. I specialize in creating precise technical documentation, visualizations, and design schematics using industry-standard software such as CorelDRAW and other design tools. My analytical mindset and problem-solving abilities allow me to approach design challenges methodically, ensuring that every project meets both aesthetic and functional standards.

I am proficient in computer operations, hardware and software troubleshooting, programming fundamentals, and technical communication in English. I thrive in collaborative team environments where I can contribute my design expertise while continuously learning from cross-functional colleagues.
